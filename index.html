Biography

My name is Mingqi Liu. I am a postdoc at USC working on seismic cycle simulation for oceanic transform faults and subduction zones.

2023.03 - Present Postdoc, USC, Advisor: Prof. Sylvain Barbot

2018.09 - 2022.12 PhD, ETH Zurich, Advisor: Prof. Taras Gerya

Research

My research focuses on the evolution of tectonic plates and the seismic cycle simulation. Using the cutting-edge brittle-ductile rheological algorithm, I systematically explore the spreading and subduction of oceanic plates.
